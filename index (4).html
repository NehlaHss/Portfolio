About

Academic background
& leadership

I am a third-year Bachelor's student in Computer Science, specializing in Information Systems and Software Engineering (ISIL), with a growing interest in web development, digital solutions, and community engagement.

Alongside my academic journey, I have developed strong leadership, organizational, and communication skills through three years of experience at Petroleum Club Skikda. I started as an active member in the Human Resources Department, later became Head of the HR Department, and then served as Vice President of Internal Relations.

I have also participated in national and international events as an organizer, participant, trainer, and representative. These experiences strengthened my sense of responsibility, teamwork, and event coordination.
